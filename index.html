About me

Hi, my name is Rui Batista and I'm a Data Enthusiast.

My background is in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a later specialization in Enterprise Data Science & Analytics. I've been working with data since I've finished my Masters degree which ammounts to approximately 8 years.

I've always enjoyed identifying patterns and extracting insights from data even before I started studying the craft. I was always mesmerized by the graphs and statistics I found on newspapers and textbooks and by the stories they were telling.

Data is everywhere nowadays, and the biggest challenges comes from collecting it and turning it into actionable value. I use tools such as Machine Learning, Data visualization and Software Engineering to accomplish that.
